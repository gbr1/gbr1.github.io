Open Source

ALFRED is open source.
Everuthing used to develop or build this project is open source.
ALFRED is going to be a community based project.

Easy to use

One of the main task of ALFRED development is simplification of use to achieve a 3-99yo target. 

Low Cost & Low Power

ALFRED devices and core are low cost.
We are working to reduce   unusefull components and lack of energy.   

Privacy Based

ALFRED systems are inside ALFRED core , so you don't need Internet connection.  You can create an "Intranet of Things" for your ALFRED network.
ALFRED doesn't monitor your lifestyle and doesn't sell your data.

Easy to build

You can build your ALFRED due its simplicity.
