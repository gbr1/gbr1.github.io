About me

I'm a Control Systems and Robotics Master Degree student. I'm attending last year at Univerisità degli Studi di Napoli Federico II.

Since 2012, I took part in Open Source world, working with NaLUG and other associations such as Officine Robotiche based in Rome.

I founded some projects, such as ALFRED (Automatic Linker For Remote Electrical Devices).

My biggest work is ERWHI, an open source robot that is used by AAEON to develop their AWS RoboMaker kit.

My life is based on music (I play ukulele), movies, comics, videogames and photography.
